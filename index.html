Project Overview

Invisible Borders is a student-led research project developed within the framework of ASEF 2026. The project explores how invisible physical and environmental barriers influence the movement, distribution, and impact of pollutants and human activity.

By combining scientific reasoning, experimental observation, and environmental awareness, the project aims to demonstrate that many critical boundaries shaping our world are not visible, yet have measurable and significant effects.
