Are You Safe?

“Ever wondered how things we cannot see move right through our backyard? Dive into the Invisible Borders of Porto Romano to discover how pollution travels and affects our environment.”

This structured 11-question survey was developed to evaluate environmental awareness, perception of pollution transport, and responsibility attribution within industrial and peri-urban contexts.

Survey Structure

The questionnaire is organized into four thematic sections:

1. Demographic Profile

  • Age group
  • School level / educational background

2. Local Awareness

  • Familiarity with the Porto Romano area
  • Awareness of fuel storage facilities

3. Perception of Environmental Risk

  • Perceived harmfulness of fuel-related pollution
  • Understanding of affected environmental compartments (air, water, ecosystems, health)
  • Knowledge of pollution transport pathways (air, water, human activity)

4. Environmental Responsibility and Engagement

  • Concern about local environmental pollution
  • Participation in environmental activities
  • Responsibility attribution for pollution reduction

The survey uses structured multiple-choice questions to ensure clarity, comparability of responses, and consistency in data interpretation.

Participation is voluntary and responses are collected anonymously for educational and analytical purposes.
