Are You Safe?

“Ever wondered how things we can't see move right through our backyard? Dive into the Invisible Borders of Porto Romano to discover how pollution travels and affects our world.”

This 11-question survey was developed to evaluate environmental awareness, perception of pollution transport mechanisms, and responsibility attribution in industrial and peri-urban contexts.

The questionnaire is structured into demographic profiling, local awareness, environmental risk perception, and environmental responsibility.

Survey Questions

  1. Age Group
  2. School Level
  3. How familiar are you with the Porto Romano area?
  4. Were you aware of the fuel storage facilities in Porto Romano?
  5. How harmful can fuel-related pollution be to the environment?
  6. Which environmental compartments may be affected?
  7. How can pollution spread to surrounding areas?
  8. Should environmental pollution be taught more at school?
  9. How concerned are you about local environmental pollution?
  10. Have you participated in environmental protection activities?
  11. Who should have the main responsibility for reducing pollution?
